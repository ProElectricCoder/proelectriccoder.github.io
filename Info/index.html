PeerLink: Privacy Policy

Welcome to PeerLink. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our peer-to-peer (P2P) messaging application.

1. How Your Messages & Files Are Handled (P2P Architecture)

PeerLink is a decentralized, peer-to-peer chat application built using WebRTC.

  • No Central Server Storage: We do not read, intercept, or store your chat messages, images, or files on our servers.
  • Direct Connections: When you send a message or file, it is transmitted directly from your device to the recipient's device. These transmissions are encrypted in transit via WebRTC's native DTLS protocols.

2. Data Storage Options

Your chat history is stored exactly where you choose to store it:

  • Browser Storage / Local Folder: Data is saved locally on your own physical device. We have no access to this data.
  • Google Drive: If you choose this option, your data is saved to a hidden application folder inside your personal Google Drive. We do not have access to your Google Drive account.

3. Information We Collect (Signaling & Authentication)

To make the P2P connection possible, we use Google Firebase. The following data is processed:

  • Account Information: If you log in using Google, GitHub, or Email, Firebase securely stores your email address, display name, and profile picture URL to authenticate you.
  • Connection Data (Signaling): To connect you with other users, our signaling server temporarily processes technical connection data (Session Description Protocol and ICE candidates). This data is deleted when it is no longer needed.

4. WebRTC and IP Addresses (Important)

Because PeerLink relies on direct peer-to-peer connections, your public IP address may be exposed to the users you are actively chatting with. This is a fundamental technical requirement of WebRTC. Do not share room codes with individuals you do not trust.

5. Third-Party Services

We utilize Google Firebase for authentication, database infrastructure (for signaling), and Google Drive API for optional backups. Your use of PeerLink is also subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
